Do you offer other activities besides ropes courses?
Yes. The company behind Sörsjöns Äventyrspark is called Äventyrsupplevelser, and we offer various packages that include teambuilding activities, pentathlon, bushcraft, climbing and canoeing, and others. You can learn more about these packages here.

Do you accept payment by card?
Yes, we accept both card and cash on site, but it's better to book in advance so you can make sure to get your spot.

Do you have multiple adventure parks? If so, which one am I going to?
Sörsjöns Äventyrspark is our main site, where we have 20 ropes courses and ziplines. We also provide other activities at "Glotterns Vildmarkspark", where we normally offer teambuilding activities. Most of the time, people will be heading to Sörsjön. Check your email booking conversation if you are unsure, or ask us.

Where are you located?
You’ll find us just north of Norrköping. Use the Google Maps road description to us to make sure you’re on the right track. As soon as you arrive to the Sörsjön camping, keep going towards the large parking lot and walk approximately 100 meters past the bathing place and to the right side of the lake.

Do we all have to climb the same course when we come as a group?
As soon as the instruction is over, you are free to split up and climb which courses you like for the next two hours. If you don’t know where to start, we will help you find a level that suits you. We usually recommend starting with a green or yellow course.

Are there changing rooms?
Yes, both by the bathing place (around 100 meters from us) and a bit further away, at the Sörsjöns Camping.

How much earlier than my booking should I arrive?
It's best to come 10 – 15 minutes before your booked time, to get all the necessary information. The instruction starts at the time you have booked, so if you arrive late you will have to wait until the next round of instructions starts (usually 30 minutes later). On busy days the start time after yours might be full.

What happens if there's bad weather?
If there's thunder in the area, all climbing will be put on hold - most other weather conditions work well as long as you've got the right clothes!

Do you have lockers for valuables?
We have simple lockers you can borrow a padlock for, but it's a good idea to leave valuables at home.

Is there a toilet on site?
There is an earth closet by the bathing place (around 100 meters from us), and a few more at Sörsjöns Camping.

Do you sell coffee?
Unfortunately, no, but in the summer, there is a kiosk by the bathing place, as well as by Sörsjöns Camping, 250 meters from us. We recommend bringing your own picnic!

Can the climbing time be extended?
During the summer period it's usually possible to extend the climbing time, especially if you tell us you want an extension as soon as you arrive. During the spring and fall seasons it might also be possible, but the safest bet is to write us an email and ask.

Can I pay by invoice?
Yes, if you're a business, school or an association. You're welcome to email your invoice details in advance!

How many courses can I climb in 2 hours?
You normally have time for between 3 and 6 courses, depending on the difficulty of each one.

Can we do a children's party with you?
For sure! The most common setup is 90 minutes climbing on the ropes courses here at Sörsjöns Äventyrspark, or rock climbing, zipline and bow-shooting at Glotterns Vildmarkspark. Both options cost 2 000 SEK and cover up to 10 participants.
